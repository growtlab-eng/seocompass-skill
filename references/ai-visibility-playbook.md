# AI visibility (AEO/GEO) playbook

AI answer engines increasingly sit between your buyer and your site. If ChatGPT
/ Gemini / Perplexity / Google AI Overviews name a competitor and not you, you
lose the customer before a click ever happens. This measures and fixes that.

## Prompt design

Generate prompts the way a *buyer* actually asks an assistant — not keywords:

- **Category best:** "best <category> tools", "top <category> software for <ICP>"
- **Comparison:** "<brand> vs <competitor>", "is <competitor> worth it"
- **Alternative:** "alternatives to <competitor>", "cheaper alternative to <X>"
- **Job-to-be-done:** "how do I <job the product does>", "what should I use to <job>"
- **Trust:** "is <brand> legit", "<brand> reviews"

Aim for 8–12 covering the categories that matter for the brand.

## What to record per prompt

- **Brand mentioned?** present / absent
- **Brand linked?** cited with a real URL (stronger than a bare mention)
- **Competitors named** and their order (first-named ≈ default recommendation)
- **Source pages** the engine pulled from (Perplexity/AI Overviews cite these) —
  these are your AEO link targets

## Share-of-Voice

```
SoV(brand) = prompts where brand appears / total prompts
```

Build the same for each competitor. The gap between you and the top competitor
is the headline number.

## Why you're not cited (and the fixes)

1. **No comparison/alternative pages** → create "<brand> vs <competitor>" and
   "<competitor> alternatives" pages. AI leans heavily on these.
2. **Claims aren't explicit/extractable** → state what you are, who you're for,
   and your differentiators in plain, quotable sentences near the top.
3. **Not in the third-party sources AI trusts** → the listicles, Reddit threads,
   G2/Capterra entries the engine cited. Get represented there.
4. **No answer-shaped content** → FAQ/H2-question structure, definition-first
   paragraphs, comparison tables — formats LLMs lift cleanly.
5. **Weak entity footprint** → consistent brand description across the web,
   schema (Organization, Product, FAQ).

## Caveats to state honestly

AI responses are non-deterministic and vary by session, region, and model
version. A snapshot shows *today*; the signal is the trend over repeated runs.
Don't present one run as definitive.
